ABOUT US

Our goal is to remedy the current concerns of pollution and accessible mobility while allowing for a variety of transportation options under an easy-to-use platform and affordable prices.

OUR SERVICES



Eco-Friendly Bus

With the use of Eco-friendly buses, we aid to maintain our local carbon footprint to a minimum and provide a more user-friendly price.

Eco-Friendly Car

If a faster solution is needed, the use of Eco-friendly cars (with a preference for electric-powered ones) is available for our users.

Eco-Friendly Bikes

For short distance travel, new methods of transport are being created everyday instead of the usual car or bus. Our suggested method is city-controlled bikes that are easily accessed at our hotspots for quick access between favoured destinations.
